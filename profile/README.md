It is based on the Enterprise edition, which means it offers advanced features, enhanced security, and comprehensive management tools.
Therefore, it provides a powerful and flexible platform for both students and educators who require a reliable and secure computing environment. Furthermore, Windows 10 Education is tailored to meet the unique needs of educational environments.

Because educational institutions often require centralized management, high-level security, and seamless collaboration tools, this edition includes features that support these requirements effectively. As a result, IT administrators can manage devices efficiently while ensuring a safe and productive digital learning experience.
